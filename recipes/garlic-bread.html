Keto Garlic "Bread"

A garlic bread for the Keto diet to accompany your Keto lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 0.25 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Step 1. Line air fryer basket with a piece of parchment paper.
  2. Step 2. Combine cheeses, egg, and garlic powder and mix til combined.
  3. Step 3. Press into a round circle on the parchment.
  4. Step 4. Heat air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and fry 10 minutes.
