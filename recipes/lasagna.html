Keto Lasagna

A lasagna for the Keto diet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 0.5 cup minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1.5 cups marinara sauce
  • 0.5 cup ricotta cheese, drained
  • 0.5 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large egg

Steps:

  1. Step 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Step 2. Cook beef with onion and garlic.
  3. Step 3. Pour in marinara and mix.
  4. Step 4. Mix cheeses and egg together & place everything in a dish.
  5. Step 5. Bake 20 to 25 minutes.
